About Nate

My name is Nate Marsh. I'm a Pittsburgh native and have lived in Chicago for the past 5 years. I attended La Roche College (now La Roche University) where I played NCAA Lacrosse as a starter all four years. My wife plays lacrosse as well, which is how we met. I also coach at Whitney Young High School. I'm currently the chef of a foodservice company that caters school lunch programs around the city. I'm a huge Star Wars nerd.
